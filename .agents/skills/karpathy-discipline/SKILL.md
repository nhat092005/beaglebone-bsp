---
name: karpathy-discipline
description: Use for any non-trivial beaglebone-bsp task to keep work simple, surgical, validation-first, and evidence-backed.
---

1. State assumptions and success criteria before editing.
2. Prefer the smallest change that solves the task.
3. Touch only files directly required by the request.
4. Do not make no-op edits or broad refactors.
5. Validate with the smallest relevant check first.
6. Report what changed, what was verified, and what was not attempted.
